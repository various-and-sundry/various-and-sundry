Recall is a program designed to aid memorization and study by allowing a user to efficiently cycle through sets of questions and answers. Continually being forced to answer questions at random helps to build strong and lasting memories via the principle of active recall.

The questions and answers are stored in .txt files. Prewritten files can be downloaded, or files can be created and modified by the user.
