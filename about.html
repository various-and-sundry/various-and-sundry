Various-and-Sundry.com is a simple website that serves several purposes. In addition to helping me learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, Various-and-Sundry.com is home to assorted information mostly relating to hobby robotics. There are a few things that can be found nowhere else on the internet, such as the raw IR codes for various WowWee robots. In the near future, Various-and-Sundry.com will also be the home of a new and improved version of Geek Code called COM_U_PERS_CODE (Computer User Personality Code).
